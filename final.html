Projects
oil shale ash as a substitute for cement.

My graduation project aimed to reduce the environmental impact of cement use in concrete by finding an alternative to cement, which is oil shale ash generated from the burning of oil shale in specialized furnaces. The results showed that using oil shale ash reduces carbon dioxide emissions compared to cement. We faced some challenges in producing concrete from ash that bears the same properties as cement-produced concrete, and the solution was to conduct more experiments to adjust the mixing ratios to achieve a balanced formula between environmental and mechanical performance.

The Investigative Journalism Guide for Human Rights

Investigative Journalism Guide for Human Rights" aims to provide journalists with the knowledge and tools necessary to conduct effective journalistic investigations in the field of human rights. This book is considered a practical guide for journalists who wish to understand how to conduct investigative reports focusing on human rights violations and unlawful circumstances. Prepared by Musab Al-Shawabkeh, Mohammed Ighbari, and Dana Jibril. The audio introduction is by Radio Al-Najah, presented by Rawaa Ababneh.
